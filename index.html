About me

I'm a software developer and an open-source enthusiast currently building highly powerful software suite at valuefy technologies for wealth management companies in and outside India. I do development as a profession and a hobby due to which I find myself building softwares in my free time to solve problems (sometimes non-existent) that could potentially help people. Due to my lack of motivation to sell, whatever I build usually goes to a cold repo in my github and in turn to the freezing artic code vault, lacking any purpose yet. Back in the college days, I, obviously with my team, had won the Smart India Hackathon 2019 edition for the problem statement "Inconsistency detection in medical annotations". During the summer internship in my sophomore year, I worked with Dr. K. Chandrasekaran at NIT Surathkal and published a research paper on "Data-Driven Stillbirth Prediction and Analysis of Risk Factors in Pregnancy". Till now, I've been tinkering with a lot of stuff in tech, dipping my fingers in all waters including but not limited to web 2.0 (which is what I do for a living), metaverse, creative-coding, machine learning, cryptocurrency etc. to the point that I can say I'm a fast learner for real but no guarantee for the memory.
